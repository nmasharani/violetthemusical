Meet the Company

Makulumy Alexander-Hills (Music Director) is a sophomore studying environmental ecology in the Earth Systems program at Stanford, and is also working towards a minor in music. A classically trained pianist, he has expanded his musical horizons to jazz and musical theatre in recent years. Having previously worked as assistant music director on Pippin as well as Ram's Head Theatrical Society's recent production of Spring Awakening, Makulumy is excited to be moving up to the music director's position with Violet. Sammi Cannold (Director) is a sophomore studying Theater, History and Education at Stanford University. She is the Artistic Director of At the Fountain Theatricals, Stanford's new musical theatre organization, and a Board Member for Ram's Head Theatrical Society. Directing credits include Ebbie and Mol (Thespis Festival, NYC), The Dining Room Play (Stanford), Annie and Joseph (Rippowam Summer Arts). Sammi has interned for The Tony Awards, Congresswoman Nita Lowey, and Beautiful: The Carole King Musical. In 2012, she was named an Intel Semifinalist for research on education in Finland. Sammi is an associate member of the Stage Directors and Choreographers Society and hopes to pursue a career in directing musicals and running a non-profit theater.

Kelly Gregg (Producer) is a Junior majoring in Human Biology with a concentration on Global Health Policy. She stage managed Spring Awakening (Ram's Head 2013), assistant stage managed Gaieties: Full Doom on the Quad (Ram's Head 2012) and acted in A Midsummer Night's Dream (Wilbur Hall Productions 2012). She feels so privileged to have her first producing experience be working side-by-side with Brandon Powell. As the Financial Manager of At the Fountain Theatricals, Kelly is excited to be a part of its inaugural production. This year Kelly is a RA in Crothers and is producing Les Misérables this Spring for Ram's Head.
